# Acme Corp — Expense Policy (v1)

1. **Meals:** max $75/person/day. Alcohol is NOT reimbursable.
2. **Hotels:** max $250/night.
3. **Flights:** economy class only for flights under 6 hours.
4. **Ground transport:** taxis/rideshare reimbursable; personal car at $0.65/mile.
5. **Receipts:** required for any expense over $25.
6. **Approval:** any single expense over $500 requires manager pre-approval (noted in the record).
7. **Weekends:** expenses on Sat/Sun are only valid if the record notes business travel.
8. **No duplicates:** the same charge may only be reimbursed once.
9. **Personal items:** not reimbursable (entertainment, gifts, clothing, personal electronics).
10. **Currency:** all amounts must be reported in USD.
